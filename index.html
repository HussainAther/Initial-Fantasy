Initial Fantasy

Welcome to Initial Fantasy!

Embark on an epic journey in the land of Glagera, where adventure awaits at every turn. In this thrilling RPG, you will take on the role of a brave hero and set out on a quest to uncover the secrets hidden within the legendary 7-11.

How to Play:

  • Choose Your Character: Start by selecting your character class from a variety of unique options, including ogres, mages, rangers, and more. Each class has its own special abilities and playstyle, so choose wisely!
  • Enter Your Name: Personalize your hero by entering your own name. This will be the name that echoes throughout the realms as you become a legendary figure in Glagera.
  • Begin Your Adventure: Once you've chosen your character and entered your name, click the "Start Game" tton to dive into the immersive world of Initial Fantasy. Prepare yourself for thrilling battles, challenging quests, and intriguing storylines.
  • Explore the World: Travel through captivating landscapes, encounter fascinating characters, and uncover hidden treasures. The land of Glagera is vast and brimming with secrets to discover.
  • Engage in Battles: Test your skills in strategic battles against formidable enemies. Use your character's unique abilities and tactics to defeat your foes and emerge victorious.
  • Collect Rewards: Gather gold, valuable items, and experience points as you progress through the game. Upgrade your equipment, learn new abilities, and unlock powerful skills to become a true hero of Glagera.
  • Complete Quests: Undertake thrilling quests and missions to unravel the mysteries of the land. Meet fascinating NPCs, make impactful choices, and shape your own destiny.
  • Customize Your Character: Enhance your hero's appearance and abilities by acquiring new armor, weapons, and magical artifacts. Tailor your character to your playstyle and make them truly unique.
  • Enjoy the Journey: Immerse yourself in the captivating storyline, rich lore, and stunning visuals of Initial Fantasy. Let the enchanting music and immersive atmosphere transport you to a world filled with wonder and adventure.

Are you ready to embark on this epic quest? The fate of Glagera lies in your hands. Brace yourself for an unforgettable adventure in Initial Fantasy!
